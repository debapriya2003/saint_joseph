PRINCIPAL DESK

It was very rightly said that ‘’Education is a shared commitment between dedicated teachers, motivated students and enthusiastic parents with high expectations’’ Hence, choosing the right school is immensely important, be it when our children first start their journey and heavily rely on their parents to identify the environment that fits them, or at any other stage of life. As one of the venerable school in this region, St. Joseph’ School, Badarpur has already made a mark of educational excellence based on firmly embedded values, a passion for learning and an environment which inspires through its sheer diversity. In keeping with our school motto ‘KNOWLEDGE FOR SERVICE’ we strive to promote patriotism, spirit of national integration, pride in our cultural heritage and a scientific outlook. Fellowship, team spirit, service and dignity of labour are given importance along with intellectual pursuits. It is a school where we teach High Performance Learning, a pedagogy that requires teachers to inculcate in our students thinking skills and a growing mind-set in an enquiry-based learning environment, to nurture and to develop their mental, social, moral and spiritual potentialities so that they grow up along with education, also as responsible members of the society. These most essential ingredients for success in the 21st century are adorned and emphasized by a rich and diverse extra-curricular activities, educational expeditions and many other pursuits, all designed to build the whole person. May all who adhere to the binding of this school, keep shining and blossom, bringing about a promising future and a decent society.
